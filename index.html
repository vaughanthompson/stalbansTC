About Us

St Albans Tennis Club is a friendly tennis club that welcomes all age groups and abilities.

The Club has six tennis Courts (with keypad code access): four Plexipave courts, two Asphalt courts. Clubrooms have great facilities and BBQ. Free onsite parking.



Hours of play

Club Day: Saturdays from 1pm until approximately 5pm (Clubroom drinks and banter if you feel like staying on).

Weekday Afternoon Tennis: Thursdays from 1.30pm.

Interclub Teams: SATC is affiliated with Tennis Canterbury 2021; we can offer Junior Interclub on Friday afternoons or Saturday mornings. Weekend Senior Interclub. Note: Interclub team registrations close in early September each season.

Junior Hotshots

Focusing on introductory concepts and tennis fundamentals. Held starting School Terms 1 & 4.

Lesson length 1 hr.
$240 for 16 weeks.
1:6 Coach/Player Ratio.

  • Red ball (5-8 years). Build fundamental and perceptual motor skills, key grips, ground strokes, serve, volley and court awareness and game understanding.
  • Orange (8-10 years). Progress stroke technique, directional hitting, serving rhythm, basic tactical awareness, and implementation.
  • Green (10-12 years). Advance tactical knowledge, spin on ground strokes and serve. 60 mins. Teen Beginner Squad. Build tactical understanding, fundamental technique on all strokes, single and doubles game understanding.
  • Teen Social (13+ years). Progress stroke technique, directional hitting, serving rhythm, basic tactical awareness, and implementation.

Adult Beginner Group

Build stroke technique, directional hitting,serving, rhythm, basic tactical awareness and implementation, scoring and doubles understanding. Held starting School Terms 1 & 4.

Lesson length 1 hr.
$240 for 16 weeks.
1:6 Coach/Player Ratio.


Adult Intermediate Group

Build consistency on all strokes, develop understanding of tactical fundamentals in doubles and singles, warm up and down routines, match routines. Held starting School Terms 1 & 4.

Lesson length 1 hr.
$240 for 16 weeks.
1:6 Coach/Player Ratio.
