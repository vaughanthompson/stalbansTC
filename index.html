Hi there. We're a friendly, social tennis club located in the Christchurch suburb of St Albans.

Returning to- new to, or just love tennis? Then we're the club for you, your family and friends.

With four hard courts and key access for members* you can play a friendly match yourselves (with a occassional guest,for small fee) or join other members for match play on our club days (see below).

Joining Information
Drop us an email or even better stop by the club during club times (Thursday evening and Saturday afternoons) for a registration form.

Membership Fees

Coaching

Junior coaching is offered after school* during school summer terms (4 & 1) from 3:30pm onwards.

Sessions are run in small groups based on age and ability for approximately one hour. Our coaching focuses on introductory concepts and tennis fundamentals.
A particular emphasis is on confidence through fun- and we encourage parents to support their kids through sideline support.

*Days / times offered are dependant on numbers registered.

Interclub - Coming in 2021!
St Albans Tennis Club will be able to submit junior and senior teams for interclub from the 2021 season onwards. Senior members will be able to form their own teams, opting in for competition. Junior members will be invited to teams with recommendations based on ability by our coaching team.

Club Times for members

Club Day - Saturdays from 1pm
A seniors club day, mainly mixed doubles, american doubles and singles as numbers allow. Wildly fun, our Saturdays are lively and energetic- punctuated with friendly club room banter!

Twilight Tennis (Summer In-season) - Thursdays from 5pm
Senior members are welcome to participate in an late afternoon tennis session.


Casual play / Guests of members

We invite you to use the courts during club times or as guests of members. The cost per session is $10 payable in our secure drop-box near the court entrance.
